Referral System Overview
Referral Levels & Percentages
  • Level 1: 5 active referrals 3%
  • Level 2: 15 active referrals 8%
  • Level 3: 30 active referrals 12%
  • Level 4: 50 active referrals 20%
  • Level 5: 50+ active referrals 25%
Important Notes
  • Only active users with investments count toward referral levels
  • Referral earnings are paid out 10 days after the investment completes
  • Users must have a withdrawal wallet set to receive referral payments
  • Admin must approve and manually process all referral payouts
